How to: Making Macaroni

Step 1:

Boil water with salt.

Step 2:

Add noodles and cook according to box.

Step 3:

After cooking, drain pasta. Then add 1/3 cup of milk to sauce pan, along with butter. Stir. Then add the noodles back in.

Step 4:

Add shredded cheese and mix until it looks cheesy enough for you. Then season with salt, pepper, onion, and garlic.

Note: If the cheese isn't mixing in, add more milk and stir.
